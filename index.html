Our Mission

We believe Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is more than a sport. It teaches confidence, respect, problem-solving, discipline, and resilience. Our goal is to make training and competition more accessible to those who need support.

Meet the Team

John Herrera

President & Founder

My name is John Herrera, and I am the President of BJJ Ultimate Call Out. I am a Purple Belt with Dark Haven Studio.

The idea for this organization came to me after I needed surgery from an injury sustained while training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. While I was fortunate enough to receive the care I needed, I realized that many members of our community are not as fortunate. Medical debt and financial hardships can prevent people from continuing their BJJ journey.

I wanted to create a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping members of the BJJ community stay on the mats through assistance programs and community support.

Our goal is to give back through gear assistance, medical relief, support for new academies, scholarships, and other opportunities that strengthen our community.

"The mat is your canvas. Your body is your chess board."
